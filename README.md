What's our project is all about:

A web/app platform that helps users find reliable and verified local technicians in one place. Users can search for electricians, plumbers, air-conditioner technicians, or other service providers in their area. The system ensures transparency, fair pricing, and trusted reviews.The platform allows users to submit service requests, compare price estimates, and choose technicians based on verified ratings and certifications.

features:
1. Verified Review System: Only customers who completed a service can leave a review, preventing fake ratings and increasing trust.
2. Price Estimation Tool: Users can see the average market price for common services before hiring, reducing the risk of overcharging.
3. Service Request & Quotation System: Users can post job requests and receive quotations directly through the platform.
4. Technician Profiles: Each provider has a profile showing certifications, completed jobs, ratings, and work history.
5. Location-Based Search: Integrated map view to easily find nearby service providers.
